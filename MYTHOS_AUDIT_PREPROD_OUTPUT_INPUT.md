## What a Just Listed Move-Up Family video shoot ACTUALLY needs the morning of

A realtor with two open house tours and a filming slot at 11am needs:
- ONE hook to read aloud / use as overlay text
- The shot list (where to point the camera, in what order, for how long)
- The CTA (what to say at the end)
- Maybe the thumbnail moment if they're shooting their own thumbnail

Everything else — the contrarian variant, the 5 B-roll suggestions with rationales, the lighting essay, the BC context paragraph — is reference, not action.
